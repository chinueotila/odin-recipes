Salmon Rice

Yes, yes. This is that tik tok trend that we've all seen and wanted to try!

Whilst I love salmon rice the recipe I am sharing may or may not match this picture shown above.

Thank you Google images.

Ingredients

  • Salmon (Freshly made or Leftover
  • Rice
  • Soy Sauce
  • Kewpie Mayo
  • Siracha
  • Snack Seaweed
  • Avocado halved and sliced (optional)
  • Orange Masego (optional)
  • Lemon slice

Instructions

  1. Place salmon on top of rice, make sure everything is warm, and break salmon apart into the rice.
  2. Add soy sauce, Siracha, and mayo on top of the rice. Add as little or as much as you like, The mix everything together.
  3. Place sliced avocado on top of rice and salmon mix. Place a small spoon of masego on top (you can add this with the sauce if you prefer it mixed into the rice).
  4. Squeeze a little lemon juice over everything.
  5. Enjoy!
