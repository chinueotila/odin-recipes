Omurice

A japanese dish with a delicious omlete served over rice.

I prefer this dish served over fried rice made from the night before.

Alternatively, if I don't have the type of rice I also like to just add it to plain white rice and get creative with the veggies I put on top.

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 Tbsp milk
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. Heat pan over medium heat and grease with a light layer of butter.
  2. Mix eggs, salt, and milk together in a bowl.
  3. Pour eggs and milk mixture into heated pan.
  4. Quickly swirl eggs with chopsticks while the eggs are set yet still runny.
  5. Now this is the hard part, use your chopsticks to try and fold the omlete. Each side being folded toward the center. It should look like an oval.
  6. Place omlete on top of your rice, and slice open dow the middle. It should open up into a delicious omlete on top.
